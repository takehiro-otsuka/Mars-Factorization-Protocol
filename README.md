# Mars Factorization Protocol: Planetary HVAC System

## Abstract
This protocol defines a deterministic, bidirectional planetary climate control system (Planetary HVAC) utilizing the extreme thermodynamics of computational exhaust heat. By refactoring the operation of massive AI Data Centers (DCs), we can simultaneously mitigate Earth's thermal/greenhouse saturation and catalyze Martian atmospheric terraforming.

## Hardware Asymmetry: The 2nm vs 28nm Paradigm
The core of this protocol relies on the physical asymmetry of semiconductor manufacturing:
*   **Earth Chips (2nm):** Optimized for extreme Flops/Watt. Reduces exhaust heat, pairs with cold-climate deployment for maximum efficiency and PUE 1.1.
*   **Mars Chips (28nm):** Legacy, thick-node architecture. Highly resistant to Martian cosmic rays. Deliberately inefficient in Flops/Watt, meaning the electrical power is optimally converted into massive thermal output (Heat/Flops) required for terraforming.

## 1. Earth Protocol: Entropy Containment
This protocol mandates the deployment of DCs exclusively in cold climates. The defining hack is the parasitic integration of Direct Air Capture (DAC) modules into the DC's exhaust system.
*   **Cost Nullification:** Air circulation energy is absorbed by DC cooling fans.
*   **Thermal Desorption:** $60^\circ\text{C} - 90^\circ\text{C}$ waste heat matches the precise thermal requirement to release $\text{CO}_2$ from DAC filters.
Captured $\text{CO}_2$ is compressed into Dry Ice and sequestered beneath the Antarctic ice sheet.

## 2. Mars Protocol: Terraforming via Compute
Taking advantage of Martian gravity ($0.38G$), DCs operate as mobile autonomous units ("Nomads"). They scan for subsurface $\text{CO}_2$ ice veins, position themselves, and utilize direct thermal contact to sublimate the ice using the massive heat output of 28nm chips.
The sublimation rate is governed by the latent heat of sublimation $L_{\text{sub}}$ and the dissipated heat $Q_{\text{dissipated}}$:

$$ \frac{dM_{\text{CO2}}(x, t)}{dt} = - \frac{Q_{\text{dissipated}}(t)}{L_{\text{sub}}} \quad (\text{if } M_{\text{CO2}} > 0) $$

## 3. Global Variance Minimization Orchestrator (GVMO)
Governs the system by minimizing the variance of utility across the entire planetary system, balancing Earth cooling, Mars heating, and Capitalist compute demands.

$$ \min \text{Var}(U) = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^{N} (U_i - \mu_U)^2 $$
